WHAT IS OUR PURPOSE?

The environment and sustainable development are very important for us. Besides the significance of green values, green energy is also a business that grows in the future: it is going to be everywhere. Saalasti wants to offer the energy industry efficient and productive tools and solutions for a better tomorrow. We are doing our part to stop the climate change.

WHERE WE ARE IN 2020?

Saalasti is a completely family owned company, which was incorporated in 1945. We are one of the world’s leading manufacturers of wood based biomass processing machines. With nearly 70 years' experience in heavy machinery and over 30 years' experience in bioenergy products we can guarantee reliability, satisfaction and easiness to our customers.
